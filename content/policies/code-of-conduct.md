Last updated: 1 January 2025

## 1. Purpose

This Code of Conduct sets out the standards of behaviour expected of all staff and representatives of Talent Fly International. We are committed to integrity, professionalism, and respect in our dealings with candidates, clients, and each other.

## 2. Scope

The Code applies to all employees, contractors, and anyone acting on behalf of Talent Fly International.

## 3. Core Values

- **Integrity:** We act honestly and do not mislead candidates or clients.
- **Respect:** We treat everyone with dignity and without discrimination.
- **Confidentiality:** We protect confidential and personal information in line with our policies.
- **Professionalism:** We deliver our services to a high standard and in line with agreed terms.

## 4. Standards of Behaviour

4.1 We do not make false or misleading statements about roles, clients, or our services.

4.2 We do not discriminate or harass. We support equal opportunity and diversity.

4.3 We avoid conflicts of interest and disclose them when they arise.

4.4 We do not offer or accept bribes or improper inducements (see Anti-Bribery Policy).

4.5 We use company and client assets responsibly and only for legitimate business purposes.

4.6 We comply with applicable laws, including employment, immigration, and data protection.

## 5. Reporting Concerns

Anyone who becomes aware of a breach of this Code should report it in line with our Whistleblowing Policy or Complaints Policy. We do not tolerate retaliation against good-faith reporters.

## 6. Consequences

Breaches may result in corrective action, training, or disciplinary measures up to and including termination.

## 7. Review

This Code is reviewed periodically. Contact: contact@talentflyinternational.com.
