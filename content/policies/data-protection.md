## 1. Purpose

This Data Protection Policy sets out how Talent Fly International ensures compliance with applicable data protection laws (including GDPR where relevant, and Singapore’s PDPA) when processing personal data.

## 2. Scope

The policy applies to all personal data processed by us, whether relating to candidates, clients, employees, or other data subjects, and to all staff and contractors who handle such data.

## 3. Principles

We process personal data in accordance with the following principles:

- **Lawfulness, fairness, transparency:** We have a valid legal basis and are transparent about how we use data.
- **Purpose limitation:** We collect data for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes and do not use it incompatibly.
- **Data minimisation:** We collect only what is necessary for the stated purposes.
- **Accuracy:** We take steps to keep data accurate and up to date.
- **Storage limitation:** We retain data only for as long as necessary.
- **Integrity and confidentiality:** We protect data with appropriate technical and organisational measures.
- **Accountability:** We can demonstrate compliance with these principles.

## 4. Roles and Responsibilities

- **Data controller:** Talent Fly International determines the purposes and means of processing for most recruitment and client data.
- **Data processor:** Where we act as processor (e.g. on behalf of a client), we process only on documented instructions and in line with this policy and applicable law.
- **Data Protection contact:** Queries and requests (e.g. access, erasure) should be sent to contact@talentflyinternational.com.

## 5. Data Subject Rights

We facilitate the exercise of data subject rights (access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection, and withdrawal of consent) within the timeframes required by law. Requests should be submitted in writing to the contact above.

## 6. Breach Management

In the event of a personal data breach, we will assess risk, contain the breach, notify affected individuals and regulators where required by law, and document the incident and remedial actions.

## 7. Review

This policy is reviewed periodically and updated to reflect changes in law or our operations. Last review date: as shown at the top of this document.
