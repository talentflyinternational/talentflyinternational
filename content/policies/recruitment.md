## 1. Purpose

This Recruitment & Selection Policy defines Talent Fly International’s approach to screening, shortlisting, and supporting the selection of candidates. We aim to deliver quality candidates to our clients in India and abroad through fair, consistent, and transparent processes.

## 2. Scope

The policy applies to all recruitment and placement activities carried out by Talent Fly International, including resume screening, shortlisting, and related verification and coordination with employers.

## 3. Principles

- **Merit-based:** Selection is based on relevant skills, experience, and fit for the role as defined by the client.
- **Consistency:** We apply the same screening criteria and standards for comparable roles.
- **Fairness:** We do not discriminate on grounds of race, gender, religion, age, disability, or other protected characteristics unless a genuine occupational requirement applies and is lawful.
- **Transparency:** We communicate clearly with candidates and clients about process and expectations.

## 4. Process

4.1 **Briefing:** We agree with the client on role requirements, must-have and nice-to-have criteria, and timeline.

4.2 **Screening:** We screen resumes and profiles against the agreed criteria. Screening may include checks for completeness, experience, qualifications, and work authorisation where relevant.

4.3 **Shortlisting:** We produce shortlists of candidates who meet the criteria and present them to the client with summaries and fit notes.

4.4 **Selection:** The client conducts interviews and makes the final selection. We may support with scheduling, feedback, and coordination as agreed.

4.5 **Placement:** Where in scope, we support offer, documentation, and joining in line with our service agreement and terms.

## 5. Quality Assurance

We periodically review our screening and shortlisting outcomes to ensure quality and consistency. Feedback from clients and candidates is considered for process improvement.
