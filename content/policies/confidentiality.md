## 1. Purpose

Talent Fly International handles confidential information relating to candidates, clients, and our operations. This policy defines how we collect, use, store, and disclose such information to protect the interests of all parties.

## 2. Scope

The policy applies to all staff and to all confidential information received or generated in the course of our business, including candidate data, client requirements, commercial terms, and internal information.

## 3. Definition of Confidential Information

Confidential information includes, but is not limited to:

- Personal data (see Privacy and Data Protection policies)
- CVs, applications, and assessment information
- Client job descriptions, salary ranges, and hiring plans
- Commercial and contractual terms
- Internal processes, strategies, and know-how

## 4. Obligations

4.1 We use confidential information only for the purposes for which it was provided or for which we have consent or another lawful basis.

4.2 We share candidate information with clients only where relevant to a role and in line with our Privacy Policy and candidate consent.

4.3 We do not disclose client or candidate confidential information to third parties except as necessary to perform our services or as required by law.

4.4 We require staff and contractors to maintain confidentiality during and after their engagement.

## 5. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect confidential information from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. Access is limited to those who need it for their role.

## 6. Retention and Disposal

Confidential information is retained in line with our Privacy Policy and legal obligations. When no longer needed, it is securely deleted or anonymised.

## 7. Breach

Any suspected breach of confidentiality must be reported promptly. We will investigate and take corrective action. Serious breaches may result in disciplinary action and, where appropriate, notification to affected parties or regulators.
