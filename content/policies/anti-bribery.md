Last updated: 1 January 2025

## 1. Purpose

Talent Fly International prohibits bribery and corruption in all its forms. This policy sets out our commitment and the standards we expect from our staff, agents, and business partners.

## 2. Scope

The policy applies to all employees, contractors, and representatives of Talent Fly International, and to our dealings with candidates, clients, officials, and third parties worldwide.

## 3. Prohibited Conduct

We prohibit:

- Offering, giving, or promising anything of value to secure an improper advantage (e.g. to win business, secure a visa, or influence a decision)
- Requesting, accepting, or agreeing to accept a bribe
- Facilitating payments or other arrangements intended to bribe others
- Failing to keep accurate records or to disguise bribes as legitimate expenses

## 4. Gifts and Hospitality

Gifts and hospitality must be modest, proportionate, and in line with local law and our Code of Conduct. They must not be intended or perceived as bribes. Staff must not offer or accept gifts or hospitality that could compromise independence or create a conflict of interest.

## 5. Third Parties

We expect agents, intermediaries, and partners to comply with anti-bribery standards. Due diligence and contractual clauses may be used where appropriate to ensure compliance.

## 6. Reporting

Suspected bribery or corruption must be reported in line with our Whistleblowing Policy. We will not tolerate retaliation against good-faith reporters.

## 7. Consequences

Breach of this policy may result in disciplinary action, termination, and reporting to authorities where required by law.

For questions or to report concerns: contact@talentflyinternational.com.
