Last updated: 1 January 2025

## 1. Purpose

This IT & Information Security Policy establishes how Talent Fly International protects information and technology assets. We aim to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of data and systems used in our recruitment and placement services.

## 2. Scope

The policy applies to all staff and to all systems, devices, and data used in connection with our business, including email, cloud services, and candidate and client databases.

## 3. Principles

- **Confidentiality:** Information is accessible only to those who need it for their role.
- **Integrity:** Data is accurate and not improperly altered.
- **Availability:** Systems and data are available when needed for business operations.

## 4. Access Control

- Access to systems and data is granted on a need-to-know basis.
- Strong passwords and, where applicable, multi-factor authentication are required.
- Access is reviewed periodically and revoked when no longer needed (e.g. on leaving the organisation).

## 5. Data Security

- Personal and confidential data is stored and transmitted using encryption where appropriate.
- Devices (laptops, phones) used for work must be secured (e.g. screen lock, encryption) and must not be used in ways that expose data to unauthorised parties.

## 6. Incident Response

Suspected security incidents (e.g. unauthorised access, malware, data breach) must be reported immediately. We will assess, contain, and remediate in line with our Data Protection Policy and any breach notification obligations.

## 7. Third-Party and Cloud Services

We use third-party and cloud services that meet reasonable security standards. Contracts and terms are reviewed for data protection and security commitments.

## 8. Training and Awareness

Staff receive guidance on their responsibilities under this policy and on recognising and reporting security risks (e.g. phishing).

## 9. Review

This policy is reviewed periodically. Contact: contact@talentflyinternational.com.
