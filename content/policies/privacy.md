## 1. Introduction

Talent Fly International ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and disclose personal data in connection with our recruitment and placement services, our website, and our communications.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to:

- Candidates who register, apply, or are referred to us for roles
- Employers and clients who use our services
- Visitors to our website and recipients of our marketing or service communications

## 3. Data We Collect

We may collect:

- **Identity and contact data:** name, email, phone, address, nationality, date of birth
- **Professional data:** CV/resume, work history, education, skills, certifications, salary expectations
- **Usage data:** how you use our website (e.g. IP address, browser type, pages visited)
- **Communications:** records of correspondence and feedback

## 4. How We Use Your Data

We use personal data to:

- Provide recruitment, screening, shortlisting, and placement services
- Match candidates to roles and share relevant information with employers (with consent where required)
- Communicate with you about roles, applications, and services
- Comply with legal and regulatory obligations
- Improve our services and website
- Send relevant updates (you can opt out of marketing at any time)

## 5. Legal Basis

We process personal data on the basis of: consent; contract performance; legal obligation; and our legitimate interests (e.g. providing recruitment services, improving our operations), where consistent with your rights.

## 6. Sharing and Disclosure

We may share data with:

- Employers and clients (for roles you apply for or are shortlisted for)
- Service providers (e.g. hosting, email, background checks) under strict confidentiality
- Regulators or law enforcement when required by law

We do not sell your personal data.

## 7. Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with law, or resolve disputes. Candidate and client data may be retained for a period after the end of the relationship for legal, compliance, or legitimate business purposes.

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have the right to: access your data; correct it; request erasure; restrict or object to processing; data portability; and to withdraw consent. You may also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise your rights, contact us at contact@talentflyinternational.com.

## 9. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

## 10. International Transfers

We are based in Singapore and may transfer data to countries where we or our clients operate. Where required, we ensure appropriate safeguards (e.g. standard contractual clauses) are in place.

## 11. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will be revised. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## 12. Contact

For privacy enquiries: contact@talentflyinternational.com.
