Last updated: 1 January 2025

## 1. Purpose

This Complaints & Grievance Policy provides a clear process for candidates, clients, and other stakeholders to raise concerns or complaints about our services, conduct, or policies. We aim to resolve issues fairly and promptly.

## 2. Scope

The policy applies to complaints about:

- Recruitment, screening, shortlisting, or placement services
- Conduct of our staff or representatives
- Application of our policies (e.g. privacy, equal opportunity)
- Any other aspect of our business that affects the complainant

## 3. How to Complain

Complaints may be submitted:

- **By email:** contact@talentflyinternational.com (subject: "Complaint")
- **In writing:** To the address published on our website or in our terms

Please include: your name and contact details; a clear description of the concern; any relevant dates, names, or reference numbers; and the outcome you are seeking.

## 4. Process

4.1 **Acknowledgement:** We will acknowledge receipt of your complaint within [X] business days.

4.2 **Investigation:** A designated person will review the complaint, gather information, and may contact you or others for clarification.

4.3 **Response:** We will provide a substantive response within a reasonable timeframe (typically within [X] business days of acknowledgement, unless the matter is complex).

4.4 **Outcome:** Our response will explain our findings and any action we will take (e.g. apology, correction, process change). Where we do not uphold the complaint, we will explain our reasons.

## 5. Escalation

If you are not satisfied with the response, you may ask for the matter to be escalated. We will advise you of the escalation process and any external recourse (e.g. regulator, dispute resolution scheme) where applicable.

## 6. Confidentiality and Non-Retaliation

We will handle complaints confidentially to the extent possible. We do not tolerate retaliation against anyone who raises a concern in good faith.

## 7. Review

This policy is reviewed periodically. Contact: contact@talentflyinternational.com.
