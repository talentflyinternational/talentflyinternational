Last updated: 1 January 2025

## 1. Purpose

Talent Fly International recognises the importance of environmental and social responsibility. This Environmental & Sustainability Policy outlines our commitment to reducing our environmental impact and operating in a sustainable and responsible manner where practicable.

## 2. Scope

The policy applies to our operations, including our use of premises, travel, technology, and procurement. We encourage alignment with this policy among our partners and suppliers where relevant.

## 3. Commitments

- **Resource use:** We aim to minimise waste and use energy and resources efficiently (e.g. paperless processes where possible, responsible disposal of equipment).
- **Travel:** We consider the need for travel and, where possible, use remote communication to reduce carbon footprint.
- **Procurement:** Where feasible, we consider environmental and ethical factors in our purchasing decisions.
- **Compliance:** We comply with applicable environmental laws and regulations in the jurisdictions in which we operate.

## 4. Continuous Improvement

We periodically review our practices and set objectives to improve our environmental performance. This policy will be updated to reflect progress and changing standards.

## 5. Communication

We communicate this policy to staff and make it available to stakeholders. We welcome suggestions for improvement at contact@talentflyinternational.com.
