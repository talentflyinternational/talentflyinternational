Last updated: 1 January 2025

## 1. Purpose

Talent Fly International is committed to equal opportunity and diversity. This policy states our commitment to non-discrimination in our recruitment, screening, shortlisting, and placement activities, and in our dealings with candidates and clients.

## 2. Scope

The policy applies to all staff and to all candidate and client interactions. It covers recruitment advertising, screening, shortlisting, and placement support.

## 3. Commitment

We do not discriminate on the basis of:

- Race, colour, or national origin
- Gender, gender identity, or sexual orientation
- Religion or belief
- Age (except where a legal requirement or genuine occupational requirement applies)
- Disability (we make reasonable adjustments where required by law)
- Marital status, pregnancy, or other protected characteristics

We comply with applicable anti-discrimination laws in the jurisdictions in which we operate (including Singapore, India, and client locations).

## 4. Positive Action

Where permitted by law, we may take positive action to encourage applications from under-represented groups. Selection and shortlisting remain merit-based and aligned with the client’s lawful requirements.

## 5. Responsibilities

All staff involved in recruitment and placement must apply this policy. Suspected breaches should be reported in line with our Complaints & Grievance Policy.

## 6. Review

This policy is reviewed periodically. Contact: contact@talentflyinternational.com.
