## 1. Purpose

This document summarises the typical structure of our Service Agreement with employer clients. The legally binding terms for each engagement are set out in the signed agreement or statement of work provided to the client.

## 2. Parties

- **Service Provider:** Talent Fly International (Singapore-based).
- **Client:** The employer or organisation engaging our services.

## 3. Services

Services may include one or more of:

- Resume screening against agreed criteria
- Candidate shortlisting and submission of shortlists
- Support with interview coordination and feedback
- Placement support (e.g. offer coordination, documentation) where in scope

The exact scope, deliverables, and timelines are specified in the individual agreement.

## 4. Fees and Payment

- Fees may be fixed per role, per shortlist, or on another basis as agreed.
- Payment terms (e.g. net 30 days, currency) are set out in the agreement.
- Additional costs (e.g. background checks, travel) may be agreed separately.

## 5. Replacement and Rebate

Where applicable, we may offer a replacement candidate or rebate if a placed candidate leaves within a defined period, in accordance with the terms of the agreement.

## 6. Confidentiality and Data

Both parties agree to keep confidential information confidential and to process personal data in compliance with applicable law. Our Privacy and Data Protection policies apply to our processing.

## 7. Liability

Our liability is limited as set out in the General Terms and in the service agreement (e.g. cap on damages, exclusion of indirect loss).

## 8. Term and Termination

The agreement will specify the term and the notice or conditions for termination. Fees for work performed and committed costs remain payable on termination.

## 9. Contact

For a copy of our standard terms or to discuss an engagement: contact@talentflyinternational.com.
