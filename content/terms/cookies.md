## 1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make sites work more efficiently, to remember preferences, and to understand how visitors use the site.

## 2. How We Use Cookies

Talent Fly International may use cookies and similar technologies for:

- **Essential operation:** To enable basic functions such as navigation and access to secure areas.
- **Preference:** To remember your settings (e.g. language, region).
- **Analytics:** To understand how visitors use our website (e.g. pages visited, time on site) so we can improve it. We may use first-party or third-party analytics tools.
- **Marketing (if applicable):** To deliver or measure relevant content or ads, where we have your consent or another lawful basis.

## 3. Types of Cookies We Use

- **Strictly necessary:** Required for the website to function. These typically cannot be disabled.
- **Functional:** Enable enhanced functionality and personalisation. You may be able to disable these.
- **Analytics/performance:** Help us understand usage. You may be able to opt out via your browser or our cookie banner if we use one.
- **Marketing:** Used for advertising or retargeting where we use such tools. We will seek consent where required by law.

## 4. Your Choices

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Note that blocking certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

Where we use a cookie banner or preference centre, you can manage your preferences there.

## 5. Third Parties

We may allow third-party service providers (e.g. analytics, hosting) to set cookies on our site. Their use is governed by their own privacy and cookie policies. We encourage you to review those policies.

## 6. Updates
