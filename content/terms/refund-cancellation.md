Last updated: 1 January 2025

## 1. Application

This policy covers refunds, cancellations, and withdrawal in relation to Talent Fly International’s services. It applies to employer clients and, where relevant, to candidates. Specific engagement terms may override or supplement this policy.

## 2. Employer Clients – Cancellation

- If you wish to cancel an engagement before we have started work, we may agree to cancel without charge or with a nominal administration fee, depending on the stage of preparation.
- Once we have commenced screening or shortlisting, fees for work performed up to the date of cancellation are generally non-refundable. Any prepaid fees for work not yet performed may be refunded or credited as agreed in your service agreement.

## 3. Employer Clients – Refunds

- We do not typically offer refunds for completed screening or shortlisting services, as the work has been delivered.
- Where we have agreed a replacement or rebate policy (e.g. if a placed candidate leaves within a guarantee period), that will be set out in your service agreement. Refunds or replacements are subject to the conditions in that agreement (e.g. notification within a defined period, reason for leaving).

## 4. Candidates – Withdrawal

- You may withdraw your application or request to be removed from a process at any time by contacting us. We will cease putting you forward for that role and will process your data in line with our Privacy Policy.
- No fee is payable by you for withdrawal.

## 5. Force Majeure

If we or you are unable to perform our obligations due to events outside reasonable control (e.g. natural disaster, pandemic, government action), we will discuss postponement, alternative arrangements, or partial refund/credit as appropriate.

## 6. Disputes

If you disagree with a fee charged or a refund decision, please contact us with your reasons. We will review and respond. Our Complaints & Grievance Policy applies to unresolved disputes.
